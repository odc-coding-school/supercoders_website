Welcome to the Orange Digital Center SuperCoders Website

SuperCoders is a training program at the Orange Digital Center that offers computer based courses for kids and teenagers.

This program empowers learners with foundamental knowledge and skills in many areas including, but not limitted to: Graphic Design, Computer Programming and Web Development.

Take A Look At Some Past Courses

1. Graphical Programming with Scratch

This course taught the fundamental of programming using a Graphical Programming Language called Scratch

2. Graphic Design with Canva

This was a 4-week course that taught the basics of graphic design using Canva where SuperCoders created stunning designs for posters, cards, presentations, and more, adding text, images, and using templates to bring their ideas to life.

How to Apply For The Next SuperCoders Course

To apply for the next course, keep following the Orange Digital Center Facebook page. The post for the next training will be posted on the ODC Facebook page along with the link to apply. When the post comes up, click on the application link to apply for your child or children
